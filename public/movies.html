Movie and Series List

My favourite HORROR movies of all time

Slasher Genre

Hostel

Three travellers backpacking across Europe meet a man who convinces them to visit a hostel with beautiful women. However, when they reach their destination, they encounter terrifying situations.

Hatchet

Stranded in the eerie Louisiana swamps, a group of tourists unknowingly faces a terrifying ancient force. Hatchet delivers heart-pounding horror, with shocking twists and suspense at every turn. This intense survival thriller is packed with gore, thrills, and nonstop terror. Are you ready to face the horror lurking in the swamp?

The hills turn red

In The Hills Turn Red, a group of friends on a hiking trip stumbles upon a remote, abandoned town with a dark, deadly secret. As they explore, they realize they're being hunted by a relentless, horrifying force. With tension building and terror around every corner, survival becomes their only hope in this chilling thriller.

Supernatural horror

Late night with devil

Late Night with the Devil takes you behind the scenes of a live TV broadcast gone horribly wrong. When a mysterious guest with dark, sinister secrets appears on a late-night show, things quickly spiral into chaos. As the night unfolds, terrifying truths are revealed, and the audience is trapped in a nightmare they never saw coming.

The autopsy of JANE DOE

In The Autopsy of Jane Doe, a father-son team of coroners are tasked with examining an unidentified woman's body found at a crime scene. As they dig deeper, they uncover eerie and inexplicable mysteries linked to the woman's death. With each chilling discovery, they realize they’re not just solving a case—they're uncovering a deadly, supernatural force.

The Black Phone

The Black Phone is a spine-chilling thriller that follows a young boy abducted by a sadistic killer. Trapped in a soundproof basement, he discovers an old, disconnected phone that allows him to communicate with the killer's previous victims. As he fights to escape, the spirits offer chilling guidance in a race against time.

Stephen King Movies

Cujo

Cujo is a gripping, tense horror story about a rabid dog terrorizing a small town. When a mother and her young son become trapped in their car by the vicious animal, they must fight for survival in the sweltering heat. With every passing minute, their chances of escaping grow slimmer, and the terror intensifies.

Creepshow

Creepshow is a thrilling anthology of horror stories, each more chilling than the last. From vengeful spirits to monstrous creatures, this film delivers a mix of creepy, twisted tales that keep you on the edge of your seat. Packed with dark humor, shocking twists, and unsettling moments, it's the perfect horror experience for fans of the macabre.

Salem's Lot

Salem's Lot (1979) is a terrifying tale of a small town overtaken by vampires. When a writer returns to his hometown, he uncovers dark secrets and realizes that the residents are falling under a sinister, supernatural influence. As the town succumbs to evil, one man must fight to save those he loves from an ancient, bloodthirsty force.
